About Thomas Ellsworth

Dubbed the “Biz Doc,” Tom Ellsworth has over 25 years of experience as a founder, CEO, president, and COO. Tom has generated over $1.8 billion in exit transactions. Tom began working with Patrick Bet-David at PHP Agency, where he served as the Chief Strategy Officer. He joined Patrick in executive roles at Bet-David Consulting and Valuetainment Investments Group.

Tom has served in executive roles at a variety of successful companies, including Sprint, where he was vice president of external incubator development, and JAMDAT Mobile Inc. (the largest wireless games publisher in the world at the time), where he served as executive vice president of marketing and corporate development prior to that company’s highly successful IPO in 2004.

Tom shares his expertise on his "Biz Doc" channel on YouTube, and through webinars, seminars, and 1-on-1 business coaching. His book, The Rat, the Race, and the Cage, details how to make great career choices and find job satisfaction.
