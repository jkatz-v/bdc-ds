About Patrick Bet-David

Patrick's family immigrated to America when he was 10 years old. His parents fled Iran at the time of the Iranian Revolution and were granted citizenship in the United States. After graduation from high school, Patrick enlisted in the U.S. military and served with the 101st Airbone before beginning his career in the financial services industry. A few years into his career with traditional companies, he was inspired to establish PHP Agency Inc., an insurance sales, marketing, and distribution company - and managed to do so before turning 30.

In 2013, Patrick founded Valuetainment, a media brand intended to teach entrepreneurship fundamentals and inspire people to break free from detrimental beliefs and limitations. In addition to providing news, other content partners, and a weekly podcast, it has been dubbed "the best channel for entrepreneurs" in the field online. Speaking on topics ranging from leadership, management and entrepreneurship, Patrick discusses the "how and why" to become an entrepreneur, as well as the importance of learning how to fully process issues.

PBD Focuses on topics including; corporate identity, leadership development at all levels, effective business strategy, critical thinking & practical, problem solving, exit strategy, multimedia network growth, compensation plans, and innovative campaign management.
